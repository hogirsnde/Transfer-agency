Who We Are

THE AGENCY
BEHIND THE
TALENT

Web Transfer Agency is an international football agency headquartered in Rotterdam, Netherlands. We are dedicated to discovering, developing, and placing football talent at clubs around the world.

Our network spans across Europe, Asia, and the Americas — giving our players unparalleled access to opportunities. We believe in long-term partnerships built on trust, transparency, and results.
